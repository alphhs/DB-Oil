COMPANY INTRODUCTION

Since 2017, "DB Oil" LLC has been operating with the aim of enhancing employment opportunities, producing high-quality products and services, developing skilled professionals, offering competitive prices in the oil industry, and ultimately establishing itself as one of the leading companies in Mongolia.

Our Company has been granted three special permits from the Ministry of Mining and Heavy Industry. These permits include the "License for Petroleum Production," issued in December 2018, as well as the "License for Wholesale Trade of All Types of Fuels" and "License for All Types of Petroleum Product Import" issued in July 2020.

Over the years, we have been operating a state-registered facility with a special permit standard, with a petroleum product capacity of 850,000 liters. We have been constantly expanding our material and technical base infrastructure and making additional investments to meet the requirements and demands of our clients.

To further expand our business in the future, our company is planning to directly import winter and summer diesel fuel that meets Euro standards. To ensure the highest quality, we are establishing partnerships with Rosneft's Angarsk Petrochemical Plant and Siberian Kupets LLC. Through this collaboration, we aim to distribute the fuel to both our existing contract customers and new clients. We provide our customers with petroleum products that meet the requirements of international - ISO, Russian Federation - GOST, and Mongolian - MNS standards and we have established a partnership agreement to work together with "Amber" LLC's laboratory, which has been accredited by the MNS ISO/IEC 17025 standard for the quality assurance of our products.

THE STORAGE FACILITY OVERVIEW

The storage facility of petroleum products was built in 2017 and in March 2020, "DB Oil" LLC acquired the following movable assets under its name after purchasing them:

  • One vertical storage tank for petroleum products with a capacity of 750 cubic meters, and two horizontal storage tanks for petroleum products with capacities of 75 cubic meters each
  • 105.9 meter long railway
  • Guard house with an area of 28 sq.m 
  • 1764 square meters land with a purpose of oil storage

STORAGE VOLUME

1. Vertical storage tank with a capacity of 700 cubic meters or 756,000 liters.

2. Two horizontal hidden tanks: Each has a capacity of 75 cubic meters and the total capacity of 150 cubic meters or 164,000 liters.

STORAGE CAPACITY

Our storage facility car stack has 3 tank car parking tracks and 3 tank cars or about 150,000 liters of product can be unloaded at the same time in 1 hour and 30 minutes.

The two loading arms of the Autostack are capable of loading 1000-1300 liters of product per minute simultaneously.

FACTORY FLOOR PLAN

THE PROJECT - FACTORY SPECIALIZING IN PROCESSING MDO, FUEL, AND PRODUCTION OF IMPROVED DIESEL FUEL

Within the framework of the project, we will establish a factory for refined final products, using eco-friendly technology, to reduce environmental degradation, decrease foreign currency outflow from Mongolia. The project aims to create a favorable environment for the domestic market by increasing job opportunities and providing sustainable employment for the country's citizens.

Project duration:  10 years

  • Estimated time to start – August 2022
  • Estimated time to complete - August 2032

TECHNOLOGY INTRODUCTION

This technology utilizes special ceramics to purify MDO and produce diesel fuel that is of superior quality compared to 100% diesel fuel. Additionally, this fuel does not deteriorate even if stored for an extended period and is eco-friendly.

The upcoming factory will utilize cutting-edge technology, featuring state-of-the-art fuel processing equipment that is both energy-efficient and eco-friendly.

The factory will be connected to the central electricity system and will utilize the "Packaged Power Station system" to provide heating and hot water, ensuring a reliable and efficient energy source for the facility.

INTRODUCTION OF THE FACTORY

Our factory, in addition to providing employment opportunities for 28 people, is committed to implementing advanced technical technologies and introducing them into production to improve efficiency. This is in line with the state policy and the objectives of the local government to promote industrial development and create a prosperous society.
